Storage Bin

About our Designs: Playing off of the design technique common to quilting in which bits and pieces of fabric scraps collected over time are pieced together to form something new and different, all of Aunt Bucky’s designs include textiles gathered from varied sources, including fabrics no longer in production and by textile artists who they themselves limit their fabric quantities and print runs. As a result the Aunt Bucky line is ever evolving and truly unique. Our collections will always include the same style selections and design sensibility, but the individual fabric prints may vary. This process is therefore somewhat improvisational and produces near one-of-a-kind pieces.
